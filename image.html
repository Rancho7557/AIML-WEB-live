1.Bounding Box Annotation

Bounding box annotation is primarily used to train automated cars to recognize obstacles on the road such as streets, vehicle, potholes, signals, and other objects. This image annotation methodology aids self-driving vehicles in identifying and comprehending their environs as well as all objects in a real-world scenario.

5.  Landmark annotation

6.  Line Segmentation

Semantic segmentation enables image classification by pixel-wise tagging of objects, allowing computer vision to identify images with dense prediction.

Semantic segmentation is a technique that visualizes multiple objects of the same class as a single entity and is primarily used for training perception models in natural environment objects.
