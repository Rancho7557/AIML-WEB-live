TextAnnotation Techniques

1.  Text Categorization

Text categorization is the initial work in the data annotation process in which the texts are classified as per various aspects like content type, purpose, ideation, & subject. Such classification is then provided to the system for future reference.

2.  Text Linguistic/Corpus Annotation

: Linguistic annotation covers any illustrative or analytic documentation applied to unrefined language data. The Linguistic annotation is used for a variety of NLP solutions to make AI datasets.

3.  Entity annotation

Entity annotation is an essential process in the age of chatbots and other NLP datasets. Our well-trained and exceptionally experienced annotation team completes the whole handful process by finding, separating, and labeling entities into text with ease.

4.   Entity Linking

Entity linking is used to further evolve search functions and client experience with the technology. Our experts link the labeled data to a URL, which has the details about the entity annotated by annotators.

5.  Sentiment Classification

Sentiment classification, also known as sentiment annotation, is used to understand sentiments, feelings, and perspectives through text. Emotional intellect is the most demanding field of AI that Smartyficial provides with high-tech facilities and experience in the field.

6.  Line Segmentation

Semantic segmentation enables image classification by pixel-wise tagging of objects, allowing computer vision to identify images with dense prediction. Semantic segmentation is a technique that visualizes multiple objects of the same class as a single entity and is primarily used for training perception models in natural environment objects.
